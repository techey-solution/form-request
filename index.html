Client Discovery Questionnaire

This version keeps only the questions a non-technical client can answer quickly. The delivery team can turn the answers into a full technical requirements document later.

How to use this
Ask for business goals, audience, must-have features, timeline, and constraints. Skip engineering jargon during discovery. This form is designed to capture just enough context for the next solution workshop.
Expected output
After submission, the responses are sent to Google Sheets and the page also generates a short discovery brief that can be shared with BA, PM, or SA.
S1
Project Basics
What the client is trying to build and who owns the request
Use plain language. Example: attract new users, educate beginners, run a competition, or launch a customer portal.
S2
Product Direction
The shape of the product without technical jargon
S3
Launch and Constraints
Timeline, audience size, geography, and business constraints
S4
Success Criteria
What matters most, and what could block delivery
